RootRotAI Overview

RootRotAI-2.1 detects Macrophomina phaseolina in chickpea images. The system utilizes two multitask transformer-based TensorFlow Lite models—one optimized for camera and root scanner modalities, and another for microscope images.

• Model 1 → optimized for camera & root scanner images
• Model 2 → optimized for microscopic pathogen detection
• Supports rapid screening and AI-assisted diagnosis
